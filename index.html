In 1989, Jotaro Kujo, the great-grandson of Jonathan and grandson of Joseph, is a Japanese high school student with a cold, stoic demeanor but deep loyalty to those he cares for. He is troubled by a mysterious power he calls an “evil spirit,” which Joseph reveals to be a Stand — a manifestation of life energy. The awakening of Stands in the Joestar bloodline is linked to the resurrection of Dio, who now inhabits Jonathan’s body. Jotaro’s Stand, Star Platinum, possesses extraordinary strength and speed. When his mother falls gravely ill due to her own Stand awakening, Jotaro embarks on a journey to Egypt with Joseph, Muhammad Avdol, Noriaki Kakyoin, and Jean Pierre Polnareff to confront Dio’s army of Stand users. Their travels span Asia and the Middle East, filled with perilous battles and heartbreaking sacrifices. In the climactic battle, Jotaro outwits Dio and overcomes “The World,” a Stand that can stop time. With Dio destroyed, his mother recovers, and the immediate threat ends — though the Joestar family’s fated struggle with Dio’s legacy is far from over.

Jotaro Kujo, the protagonist of JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure: Stardust Crusaders, is one of the most iconic figures in the series. With a stoic and silent demeanor, Jotaro often appears aloof or impatient, yet beneath this exterior lies deep loyalty and compassion. When it matters most, he never hesitates to put himself in harm’s way for his friends and family. His Stand, Star Platinum, is renowned for its extraordinary strength, speed, and precision, eventually awakening the ability to stop time. This power not only secured his victory over Dio in their climactic battle but also cemented his reputation as an unmatched warrior. Jotaro’s sharp intellect and calm under pressure allow him to turn the tide even in hopeless situations, while his unshakable sense of duty makes him one of the most admirable heirs of the Joestar bloodline.
